Chemical Nomenclature Practice

Choose a practice type, then either name the species or write its formula. Enter subscripts as regular numbers (for example, CaCl2) and charges using ^ symbol when needed (for example, Fe^3+). For +1 and -1 ions, the ^ symbol is not needed. For hydrates, use the asterisk * as the center dot (for example, KNO3*2H2O).
